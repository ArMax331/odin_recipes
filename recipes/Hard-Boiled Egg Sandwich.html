These are so good! I have been making these hard-boiled egg sandwiches for years and we absolutely LOVE them. This is a great on-the-go breakfast or just a different way to use up leftover hard-boiled eggs. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  1. 2 slices white bread, toasted
  2. 1 tablespoon butter, softened
  3. 2 tablespoons whipped cream cheese
  4. 1 hard-boiled egg, sliced
  5. 5 dashes hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's RedHotÂ®), or to taste
  6. 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Butter each slice of toast. Spread 1 tablespoon cream cheese onto each slice.
  2. Layer hard-boiled egg slices evenly onto 1 slice of toast. Add hot sauce, salt, and pepper. Top with other slice of toast.
